Ducks are the coolest animal out there. They fly, go on land, and swim! What a tri-fecta!! what else would you want in an animal!

Geese are basically just like ducks, except they're bigger and make more noise. Geese are probably one of the most annoying types of birds. If you've ever been to a park after a gaggle of geese was there, you know what I'm talking about. But don't trash talk them when they're around, because geese do not mess around. They will attack you!

Pandas are all about the Eucalyptus; it's their go to munchies! They look like they're wearing a mask all the time, but it's not because they're doing anything shady. The giant panda spends about two-thirds of its day feeding and the remainder resting. What. A. Life.
